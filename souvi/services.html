Our Techniques

Four Bending Methods

We deploy the right bending technique for each geometry to achieve optimal quality and precision.

01
Most precise
Tight radius Low distortion CNC-controlled

Rotary Draw Bending

Pipe is clamped against a rotating bend die and drawn around it. Produces tight-radius, repeatable bends with minimal wall distortion — ideal for seat frame main structures.

02
High volume
Moderate radius Cost-effective High throughput

Compression Bending

Pipe stays stationary while a slide piece pushes it around a fixed bend die. Cost-effective for moderate-radius bends in larger production volumes.

03
Large radius
Large radius Structural tube Repeatable

Ram Bending

A ram presses the pipe over a radius block while counter-rollers support the sides. Best for large-radius bends in structural tube components with high repeatability.

04
Arc & ring bends
Arcs & circles Smooth curves Back structures

3-Roll Bending

Three rollers progressively form the pipe into smooth curves. Effective for arcs and circular bends in seat frame back structures and large tubular rings.
